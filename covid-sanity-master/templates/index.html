bioRxiv (pronounced "bio-archive") is a free online archive and distribution service for unpublished preprints in the life sciences.

It is operated by Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, a not-for-profit research and educational institution. By posting preprints on bioRxiv,

authors are able to make their findings immediately available to the scientific community

and receive feedback on draft manuscripts before they are submitted to journals.
